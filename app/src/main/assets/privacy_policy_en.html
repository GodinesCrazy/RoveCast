Privacy Policy — RoveCast

Last updated: August 14, 2025

1) Who we are

RoveCast is a mobile application that lets you discover and listen to online radio stations. If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact: ivanmarty5338@gmail.com.

2) Scope

This notice explains what information RoveCast processes, how and why we use it, and your choices. It applies to the app and any in-app experiences (e.g., favorites, sleep timer, alarm, Chromecast casting).

3) What data we collect

  • On-device preferences (stored locally on your device): favorites, last station played, sleep timer and alarm settings.
  • App usage data (aggregate/technical): events like screen views, taps, errors, crash diagnostics, and performance metrics (only if such diagnostics/analytics are enabled in the build).
  • Device and network data: device model, OS version, language, country/region, IP-derived coarse location, and advertising identifier (for ads and fraud prevention).
  • Streaming/Directory lookups: to play a station, your device connects to third-party stream or directory servers, which see your IP address and user-agent; they process this under their own privacy policies.

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not intentionally collect your name, email, or phone number within the app.

4) How we use data

  • Operate and improve core features (playback, favorites, alarm/sleep timer, station search).
  • Ensure reliability, security, anti-abuse, and measure performance at an aggregate level.
  • Show advertising in the free tier and manage frequency capping.
  • Enable casting via Google Cast where available.

5) Third-party services we use

RoveCast integrates third-party SDKs/services that may process data under their own terms:

  • Google AdMob (ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention). Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Cast SDK (Chromecast). Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Online radio directories/stream providers used to discover and play stations (each station/directory has its own policy).
  • (Optional) Analytics/Crash reporting such as Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics if enabled in your build. Check Google’s policy above.

These partners may use device identifiers, cookies/SDK storage, and IP-based location for ad delivery, analytics, and fraud prevention.

6) Legal bases (where applicable, e.g., GDPR)

  • Legitimate interests: providing and improving the app, securing the service, measuring performance.
  • Consent: personalized advertising and certain analytics features where required by local law. When consent is requested, you can accept or decline; you can also change preferences later if the SDK provides controls.

7) Your choices and controls

  • Advertising choices: use your device settings (e.g., “Reset advertising ID”, “Opt out of Ads Personalization”).
  • In-app consent: where provided, adjust your ad/analytics preferences via the app or the SDK’s dialogs.
  • Clear local data: from system settings, you can clear the app’s storage or uninstall the app.
  • Access/erasure requests: email ivanmarty5338@gmail.com. We will coordinate with relevant processors when applicable.

8) Data retention

  • On-device preferences remain until you clear the app’s data or uninstall.
  • Aggregate diagnostics/analytics retained by third parties follow their own schedules.

9) Children’s privacy

RoveCast is not directed to children under the age required by local law for personalized ads (e.g., under 13). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10) International transfers

Third-party providers may process data in countries other than yours, subject to their safeguards and privacy policies.

11) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

12) “Do Not Sell or Share” (California)

Depending on how ads/analytics are configured, some processing may be considered a “sale” or “sharing” under California law. You can limit this via device settings and any in-app consent tools provided.

13) Changes to this policy

We may update this notice from time to time. The “Last updated” date shows the most recent change. Material updates will be highlighted in-app.

14) Contact

For questions or requests about this policy, contact ivanmarty5338@gmail.com.

This notice is provided for transparency and does not constitute legal advice.
